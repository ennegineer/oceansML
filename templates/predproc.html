Steps we took

  • Filtered by country first
  • Then by year to include 1990 to current
  • Most columns dropped: only species, area, time and amount of fish were kept
  • 'Get_Dummies' to split up the species and areas
  • Train Test Split
  • Two diverging baths: Random Forest Regressor and Random Forest Classifier
  • Regressor = exact, Classifier = range
  • China: low amount of unique values allows for the use of regressor
  • Classifier: Bins were made with sklearn.KBinsDigitizer.
  • Results for both: very similar predictions across the board. Regression had more diversity. Classifier despite trying various techniques still was very generalized.
